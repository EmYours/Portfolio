About Me

Hello! I'm James Angelo Bautista, a passionate web designer and developer dedicated to creating clean, modern, and user-friendly websites and applications. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java, and I enjoy building responsive and interactive digital experiences that work seamlessly across all devices.

I also have hands-on experience with Android Studio and have developed several mobile applications with a focus on usability and performance. I'm continuously learning and improving to stay updated with the latest technologies in the field.

In addition to software development, I’m skilled in building, troubleshooting, and repairing computer hardware. Whether it's assembling PCs, diagnosing hardware issues, or optimizing system performance, I can handle technical tasks efficiently.

I am also knowledgeable in networking—capable of setting up, configuring, and maintaining wired and wireless network connections. I understand how to create stable and secure network environments for both home and small business setups.

I value clean design, efficient code, and a user-focused mindset in everything I build. When I'm not coding or fixing hardware, I enjoy exploring new tech tools, learning new skills, and challenging myself with creative projects.
